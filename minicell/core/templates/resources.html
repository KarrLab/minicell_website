MolliGen: Mollicutes comparative genomics database

MolliGen is a database focused on Mollicutes comparative genomics. MolliGen provides annotated sequence data and analysis for 56 genomes belonging to 37 species. MolliGen provides genome and genetic element pages, homology relationship defined by various methods, pre-computed statistics, metabolic pathways, and a huge collection of tools for exploring and comparing genomes.

Mycoplasma pneumoniae whole-cell model (Coming soon)

Whole-cell computational model of M. pneumoniae which represents 27 cellular pathways, accounts for every annotated gene function, and predicts the dynamics of every molecular species over the entire cell cycle. Provides all MATLAB simulation and simulation analysis code.

MyMpm: The Mycoplasma pneumoniae database

MyMpn is an online resource which contains numerous experimental and computational omics-scale Mycoplasma pneumoniae datasets generated by an international collaboration between the The Design of Biological Systems Group at the CRG and sevaealh groups at the EMBL Heidelberg. MyMpn includes gene expression, gene essentiality, protein abundance, protein complexes composition, metabolic flux, growth, and 3D tomography data. MyMpn provides an intuitive web interface to this wealth of data.

WholeCellKB: Mycoplasma pneumoniae knowledge base (Coming soon)

Web-based database of M. pneumoniae molecular biology and physiology including including:

  • Cellular chemical composition,
  • Growth medium composition,
  • Gene locations, lengths, and directions,
  • Transcription unit organization and transcriptional regulation,
  • Macromolecule composition,
  • Reaction stoichiometry, kinetics, and catalysis, and
  • Extensive links and cross-links to all references used to construct each model organism database.
